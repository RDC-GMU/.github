# George Mason University - Raytheon Drone Competition (RDC)

Welcome to the GitHub Organization of the **Raytheon Drone Competition (RDC)** team at George Mason University.

---

## About the Team
The RDC Team is currently focused on developing collaborative autonomous systems for the **2025-2026 Raytheon Autonomous Vehicle Competition (AVC)**.

- **Mission:** To design, integrate, and test an autonomous UAV and UGV system capable of collaborative target identification and dynamic landing in GPS-denied environments.
- **Location:** MARC @George Mason University, Fairfax, VA.
- **Meeting Times:**
    - Tuesdays: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
    - Thursdays: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
    - Fridays: 1:00 PM - 4:30 PM
